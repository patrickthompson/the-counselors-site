Most lives don't collapse.
They drift.

Not all at once. Not dramatically. One domain quietly falls behind while you're focused on another. Your health slips while you're building your career. Your relationships thin while you're fixing your finances. Your mind stagnates while your body is fine.

No single failure. Just a slow, unmonitored decline across the things that matter most.

The Counselors is a system designed to make drift impossible.

Inward Outward

The outward domains rely heavily on personal interactions and connections. You can do everything right and still fail — because they depend on the whims and wishes of others. That's not a bug. It's the structural truth of human connection.

Each domain has a dedicated Counselor — a structured AI session that runs the same rigorous protocol, tailored to its domain.

Three Tiers

Tier 1 — Domain
The Counselors

One per domain. Runs the 9-step protocol. Diagnoses, defines mission and vision, identifies tensions, scores health, assigns concrete actions. The structured mirror you never had.

Tier 2 — Coordination
The Supervisor

Oversees all nine counselors. Maintains the action registry and follow-through monitoring. "Is the overall structure of this life moving toward stability, growth, and coherence?"

Tier 3 — Integration
The Council Meeting

All nine domains report in. Structured round-robin. The format that creates accountability and surfaces cross-domain conflicts. Where the whole picture becomes visible.

The Protocol

Every counselor session follows the same nine steps. No improvisation. No drift.

  1. Where I Stand — honest diagnostic of current state
  2. Mission — what is this domain fundamentally for?
  3. Vision — at age 85, what would "handled well" look like?
  4. Principles — non-negotiable identity-level guardrails
  5. Structural Tensions — contradictions and trade-offs
  6. Literature Review — frameworks that clarify thinking
  7. Metrics — health score (0–10) + trajectory (−2 to +2)
  8. Immediate Actions — specific, realistic, measurable
  9. Cadence — when do we meet again?

The System

The Counselors is not a book. It's not an app. It's an integrated platform designed to work together.

Structured counselor prompts for every domain. Run them with Claude, ChatGPT, or any AI tool. The protocol does the heavy lifting. The AI enforces structure and prevents drift within sessions.

Others working through the same system. Accountability partners, shared progress, and the knowledge that someone else is doing the hard work alongside you. Drift is harder when someone is watching.
